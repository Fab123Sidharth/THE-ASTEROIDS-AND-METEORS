THE ASTEROIDS AND METEORS

Asteroids are the large rocky stones which lie in Asteroid Belt ,in between of "Mars" and "Jupiter".

Asteroids range in size from Ceres – the largest at about 1003 kilometers in diameter – to bodies that are less than 33 feet (10 meters) across. The total mass of all the asteroids combined is less than that of Earth's Moon.

Meteors are small objects made up mainly of stones present in space. They regularly enter the Earth's atmosphere. As a Meteor enters the Earth's atmosphere at a high speed , it is heated by friction with air, and in most cases it burns to ashes in a very short time. As it heats up and burns, it apppears as a glowing streak of light and is called a shooting star.

If a meteor is large, it may fall on the Earth's surface before being completely burnt out. Such meteors are called meteorites.
